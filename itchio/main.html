Mechapod

You are stranded on a dead planet that's been taken over by ancient technology.

By reverse engineering their craft, you must use your time and the scraps you find around to get a Golem started and hack more Frames, that will ensure your survival.

Will you live to see the light of another day?

Only your skills in the battlefield will tell!

Controls

Move & Aim
🖱️ Mouse
📱 Drag Touchscreen
Ability
🖱️ Left Mouse Button
📲 Tap Touchscreen

How to play

Use your mouse 🖱️ (or touchscreen 📱) to lead your Frames into battle! Collect scrap to buy upgrades, and beat the waves of enemies. There's a mini-boss too, and we're planning on adding a full-on Boss after the jam!
